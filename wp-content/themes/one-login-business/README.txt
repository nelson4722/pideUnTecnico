== Description ==

One Login Business is a feature-rich, robust, elegant and intuitive business WordPress theme for business, freelancing and corporate company to make a mark and settle business in the online market. Its beautiful layout design will give you much space to showcase all your services in the most professional manner to gain customers’ trust. It is a great theme for eCommerce website, enterprise, corporate house, product promoting company, investment, sales and marketing firm, start-up, financial advisor, legal consultants, accountants, bitcoin and cryptocurrency business, brokers, trading business, business developing agency and similar websites. One Login Business has a responsive layout and cross-browser compatibility that makes it comfortably adjust on all screen sizes and load on all browsers respectively. It is multilingual, retina ready and supports RTL writing; its pages are optimized to load with lightning speed. Its layout can be set into three different formats: boxed, full-width and full screen. This business WordPress theme allows you to customize it at its full capacity without requiring any previous coding knowledge. It has plenty of social media icons and its SEO will put your website at a higher rank in search engines. It has a gliding navigation and user-friendly interface making it an ideal business theme.

== Installation ==

Step 1: Add new page as "Home" select page attribute as "custom home page" publish it.

Step 2: Go to Customizer >> Homepage settings >> select static page >> and then select the home page >> publish it.

Step 2: GO to Dashboard >> Appearance >> Custiomize >> Theme Settings >> Here you wil get all the settings to setup home page.

Usage:

Its important to know that the slider will work if your post and pages has featured image.
